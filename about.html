The Beginning

Dosa House wasn't built in a day. It started with a simple vision: to bring the authentic, home-cooked flavors of South Indian grandmother's kitchens to the streets of Bhimavaram. We realized that in the rush of modern life, the art of slow-fermented batter and hand-pounded spices was fading away.

So, we decided to go back to the roots. No shortcuts, no preservatives‚Äîjust pure ingredients and patience.
